MEDICAL REFERENCE KNOWLEDGE BASE

Blood Sugar:
- Normal fasting: 70–99 mg/dL
- Prediabetes: 100–125 mg/dL
- Diabetes: ≥126 mg/dL

HbA1c:
- Normal: below 5.7%
- Prediabetes: 5.7–6.4%
- Diabetes: ≥6.5%

Hemoglobin:
Male: 13.8–17.2 g/dL
Female: 12.1–15.1 g/dL

Cholesterol:
Total:
- Normal: <200
- Borderline: 200–239
- High: ≥240

LDL:
- Optimal: <100
- High: ≥160

HDL:
- Low: <40
- Good: ≥60

Blood Pressure:
Normal: <120/80
Stage 1: 130–139/80–89
Stage 2: ≥140/90

CRITICAL CONDITIONS:
- HbA1c above 9% = uncontrolled diabetes
- Hemoglobin below 8 = severe anemia
- Glucose above 300 = emergency
